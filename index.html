Gen Z

Emotionally invested digital natives, balancing expression with self-awareness
Gen Z see digital media as a space for identity, creativity and connection. They're confident with new tech and responsive to representation, but increasingly reflective about the emotional impact of social platforms. Rather than switching off, they actively manage their media use and expect relevance, transparency and value in return for attention or data.
  • Strong attachment to social and creator-led platforms
  • High sensitivity to authenticity, inclusion and tone
  • Open to data use when the value exchange is clear
